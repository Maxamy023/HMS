About Hozar Marine

25 years of excellence in marine equipment and services

Our Story

Founded in 1998, Hozar Marine has been a trusted provider of high-quality marine equipment and solutions for over 25 years. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide marine professionals with reliable, innovative products that withstand the harshest ocean conditions.

What started as a small supplier to local fishing fleets has grown into an international company serving commercial shipping, naval operations, yacht builders, and recreational boaters worldwide.

Today, we're proud to be recognized as an industry leader known for our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Our Approach

At Hozar Marine, we believe that success on the water depends on equipment you can trust. That's why we subject all our products to rigorous testing in real-world conditions before they ever reach our customers.

We work closely with naval architects, marine engineers, and experienced seafarers to develop solutions that address the actual challenges faced at sea. Our collaborative approach ensures that every product we offer delivers exceptional performance, reliability, and safety.

From advanced navigation systems to essential safety equipment, each product in our catalog represents our unwavering commitment to excellence and innovation.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide marine professionals and enthusiasts with innovative, reliable equipment that enhances safety, performance, and efficiency on the water.

Our Vision

Our Values

Quality, innovation, reliability, and customer satisfaction are the core values that guide everything we do at Hozar Marine.
